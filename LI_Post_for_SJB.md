Do you miss thinking?

Not consuming information. Not attending panels. Thinking — out loud, together, about something that actually matters.

I've spent 28 years building a public knowledge graph called [Jerry's Brain](jerrysbrain.com) (using software called TheBrain). Over half a million connected nodes. Everything from [trust models](https://bra.in/4jY6RA) to [useful reframes](https://bra.in/8px2RK) to [why Britannica failed](https://bra.in/6jkxAw)(it wasn't Wikipedia) and [Netflix didn't](https://bra.in/7pWAko).

I've turned it into a live session format for virtual events: [Stump Jerry's Brains](https://stumpjerrysbrains.com/).

The format is simple. Your audience challenges me with any topic — live, unscripted. I navigate my external Brain in real time, narrating what I find. Threads connect. Surprises emerge. The session's shape is determined entirely by what the room actually cares about.

No slides. No prepared arc. Entirely unrepeatable.
