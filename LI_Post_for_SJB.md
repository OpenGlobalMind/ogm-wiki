Do you miss thinking?

Not consuming information. Not attending panels. Not outsourcing it to AI. Thinking — out loud, together, about something that actually matters.

Like a squirrel obsessively stashing nuts for the winter, I've spent 28 years happily curating a knowledge graph called [Jerry's Brain](jerrysbrain.com) (using [TheBrain](https://thebrain.com/) software). It's open to the public, with over half a million connected nodes, including everything from [trust models](https://bra.in/4jY6RA) and [useful reframes](https://bra.in/8px2RK) to [Angine de Poitrine](https://bra.in/6vALE3) and the [lovefest around Agentic AI](https://bra.in/6jkeaZ). 

What's fun, though, is talking over my Brain. My external Brain gives me superpowers no other human has, and I'd like to share them with you.

Finally, I've turned it into a live session format for virtual events: [Stump Jerry's Brains](https://stumpjerrysbrains.com/). SJB is part knowledge art, part escape room, part Red Team for your beliefs.  

The format is simple. Your group challenges me with *any topic*, live and unscripted. I navigate my external Brain in real time, weaving and narrating my reply. Threads connect. Surprises emerge. The session's shape is determined entirely by what the room actually cares about.

No prepared arc. No net. Entirely bespoke and unrepeatable.
