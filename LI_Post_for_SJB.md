Do you miss thinking?

Not consuming information. Not attending panels. Not outsourcing it to AI. Thinking — out loud, together, about something that actually matters.

I've spent 28 years happily curating a knowledge graph called [Jerry's Brain](jerrysbrain.com) (using TheBrain software). It began as my personal second brain, but when TheBrain enabled public sharing, I jumped on it. Over half a million connected nodes. Everything from [trust models](https://bra.in/4jY6RA) to [useful reframes](https://bra.in/8px2RK) to [why Britannica failed](https://bra.in/6jkxAw)(it wasn't Wikipedia) and [Netflix didn't](https://bra.in/7pWAko).

Finally, I've turned it into a live session format for virtual events: [Stump Jerry's Brains](https://stumpjerrysbrains.com/). SJB is part knowledge art, part escape room, part Red Team for your beliefs. My external Brain gives me superpowers no other human has, and I'd like to share them with you. 

The format is simple. Your group challenges me with any topic, live and unscripted. I navigate my external Brain in real time, weaving and narrating my reply. Threads connect. Surprises emerge. The session's shape is determined entirely by what the room actually cares about.

No slides. No prepared arc. No net. Entirely unrepeatable.
