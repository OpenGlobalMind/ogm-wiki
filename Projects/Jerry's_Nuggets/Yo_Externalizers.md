# Yo, Externalizers! 
  
*Let's share our data better.* 

<iframe width="560" height="315" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/ArUdB1zH9ew?si=B-SKyQ5b7A6KYUy-" title="YouTube video player" frameborder="0" allow="accelerometer; autoplay; clipboard-write; encrypted-media; gyroscope; picture-in-picture; web-share" referrerpolicy="strict-origin-when-cross-origin" allowfullscreen></iframe>

If we’re all going to make better decisions together, we may have to share the state of our beliefs more explicitly.

Why did the Global Financial Crisis of 2008-2009 happen? Look [here](https://bra.in/7j3PNr). Why so many protests around the world the last few decades? Look [here](https://bra.in/2jkMaZ).

As you likely know by now, I’ve been actively sharing what I see and how I think it all fits together through [my online Brain](http://jerrysbrain.com/) (using [TheBrain](http://thebrain.com/) software) for over a quarter century. It’s lonely work, actually, but doesn’t have to be.

I’m on a quest to find more “Externalizers,” whatever their software preferences. I’m also eager to find a better term for us quirky over-sharers.

If you’re one, please get in touch. More importantly, if you suspect one of your friends or colleagues is one, please forward this to them.

Generative AI will either put our passions out of play entirely, or make Externalization more important. I’d like to find out which way this plays out, and be helpful along the way.
