# Connections Inspired Me
 
*Remember the BBC/PBS series?* 

<iframe width="560" height="315" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/-NhhEnHAnlQ?si=NxcRx38ryu2KRz87" title="YouTube video player" frameborder="0" allow="accelerometer; autoplay; clipboard-write; encrypted-media; gyroscope; picture-in-picture; web-share" referrerpolicy="strict-origin-when-cross-origin" allowfullscreen></iframe>

[This memorable clip](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=2WoDQBhJCVQ) of James Burke explaining how we got to firing rockets into space will remind you what the [Connections](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Connections_\(British_TV_series\)) series was like. I remember being transfixed when I first watched the series on TV (remember TV?) as a youth.

I loved the notion that observant people through history had noticed how chocolate plus peanut butter from seemingly unrelated fields could lead to Earth-shaking innovations. Burke is a gifted storyteller. (For some of my other influences, watch [this video](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Bev9VChj5xQ&feature=youtu.be) I posted in 2016.)

Reader, I’m willing to bet that you were inspired by one of these:

- [Lord of the Rings](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Lord_of_the_Rings)
- [Ayn Rand](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ayn_Rand)
- [Star Trek](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Star_Trek)
- Ray and Charles Eames’ [Powers of Ten](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0fKBhvDjuy0) video
- Carl Sagan’s [Cosmos](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cosmos:_A_Personal_Voyage)
- The “[Blue Marble](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Blue_Marble)” photo of Earth from space

It shouldn’t surprise you that I have such a collection [in my Brain](https://bra.in/5pxdW6), where you may find other items that have had similar effects.

Of course, I’d love to know what inspired you. Please lmk in the comments; I’ll add novel items to my Brain.
